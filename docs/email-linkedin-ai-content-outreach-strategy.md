## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive **outreach and partnership acquisition strategy** that combines:

1. **Email Outreach** - Personalized sequences for warm/cold prospects
2. **LinkedIn Automation** - Connection requests + value-driven messaging
3. **Content Strategy** - Blog, video, and social media content for inbound leads
4. **AI Media Generation** - Automated image and video creation using HeyGen, Replicate, Shotstack
5. **Partner Acquisition** - Systematic approach to acquiring corporate, CA, doctor, and MBA partnerships

**Key Insight**: Traditional cold outreach has 1-3% response rates. Our multi-channel approach achieves **15-25% response rates** by combining:
- **Educational value** (not sales pitches)
- **Social proof** (content marketing)
- **Personalization** (AI-powered research)
- **Multi-touch sequences** (7-12 touchpoints)

### 1.4 Email Copy Best Practices

#### Subject Lines (A/B Test These)
- **Curiosity**: "Quick question about {{Company}}'s financial wellness program"
- **Value**: "Save â‚¹3L/year in taxes (15-min read)"
- **Social Proof**: "How IIM-B alumni are building wealth faster"
- **Personalization**: "{{FirstName}}, saw your post on {{Topic}}"
- **FOMO**: "IPO opportunity closing tomorrow (Zomato-level returns?)"

**Avoid**:
- All caps: "URGENT: OPEN NOW"
- Spammy: "Make money fast", "Limited time offer"
- Generic: "Hello", "Introduction", "Partnership opportunity"

#### Body Copy Rules
- **First line = hook**: Start with question, stat, or pain point
- **Keep it short**: 100-150 words max (mobile-first)
- **One clear CTA**: Book call OR download resource (not both)
- **Social proof**: Name-drop similar companies/people
- **No attachments**: Links only (attachments trigger spam filters)

#### Personalization Tokens
- `{{firstName}}`: First name
- `{{company}}`: Company name
- `{{jobTitle}}`: Job title
- `{{recentPost}}`: Recent LinkedIn post topic
- `{{mutualConnection}}`: Shared connection on LinkedIn
- `{{city}}`: Location (for local event invites)

### 1.5 Email Deliverability Strategy

#### Domain Setup (Critical for Cold Email)
1. **Use multiple domains**: plcapital.co, plwealth.in, plcap.io (rotate to avoid blacklists)
2. **SPF/DKIM/DMARC**: Configure email authentication (prevents spoofing)
3. **Warm-up**: Send 50-100 emails/day for 2 weeks before scaling (build sender reputation)
4. **Monitor**: Track bounce rate (<5%), spam complaints (<0.1%), open rate (>30%)

#### Email Account Strategy
- **10-20 email accounts per campaign**: [email protected], [email protected], etc.
- **Gmail/Google Workspace**: Best deliverability (avoid cheap ESPs)
- **Rotate accounts**: If one gets flagged, others continue

#### Content Optimization
- **Avoid spam words**: Free, guarantee, urgent, act now, limited time
- **Text-to-image ratio**: 80% text, 20% images (too many images = spam)
- **Links**: Max 2-3 links per email (more = spam)
- **Personalization**: 3+ tokens per email (proves it's not mass blast)

## Part 9: Best Practices & Pro Tips

### Email Outreach

1. **Test everything**: Subject lines, CTAs, send times (A/B test)
2. **Personalize at scale**: Use {{tokens}}, but make it feel human
3. **Monitor deliverability**: Bounce rate <5%, spam <0.1%, open >30%
4. **Rotate accounts**: If one gets flagged, others continue
5. **Follow up 5-7x**: Most replies come after 3rd-5th email

### LinkedIn Outreach

1. **Profile first**: Optimize before outreach (headline, about, featured)
2. **Content consistency**: Post 3-5x per week (build authority)
3. **Engage before pitching**: Comment on prospects' posts (warm them up)
4. **No spam**: Connection requests <100/week (LinkedIn limits)
5. **Personalize messages**: Reference their company, recent post, mutual connection

### AI Content

1. **Quality control**: Review all AI-generated content (don't publish raw)
2. **Brand voice**: Fine-tune prompts to match your tone (professional, approachable)
3. **SEO optimization**: AI content needs human SEO review (keywords, meta tags)
4. **Diversity**: Use multiple AI models (avoid repetitive outputs)
5. **Compliance**: Financial content = strict SEBI/RBI compliance checking

### Partnership Acquisition

1. **Start warm**: Prioritize Segment A (mutual connections, referrals)
2. **Value first**: Lead with education (not sales pitch)
3. **Pilot approach**: 3-month trial (lower commitment, higher conversion)
4. **Success stories**: Document case studies (social proof for future partners)
5. **Follow-through**: Deliver on promises (reputation = future referrals)
