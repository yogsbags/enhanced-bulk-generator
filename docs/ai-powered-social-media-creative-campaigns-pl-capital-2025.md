**Key Philosophy**: Traditional financial advisory content is boring (stock charts, corporate headshots, text-heavy posts). Our approach:

1. **Visual-First** - Every post starts with eye-catching AI-generated imagery
2. **Story-Driven** - Client transformations, not just financial jargon

4. **Platform-Native** - Optimize for each platform's algorithm & user behavior
5. **Conversion-Optimized** - Every creative drives to lead magnet/calendar booking

**Expected Results** (Year 1):

- **5,000 LinkedIn followers** ‚Üí 500 qualified leads

### 9.2 A/B Testing Framework

**What to Test**:

**Visual Elements**:

- Image style (AI-generated vs stock vs real photos)
- Color schemes (green vs blue vs gold)
- Text overlay (bold vs subtle)
- Face vs no face in images
- Lifestyle vs abstract vs financial graphics

**Copy Elements**:

- Hook types (question vs stat vs story)
- Post length (short vs medium vs long)
- CTA placement (beginning vs middle vs end)
- Emoji usage (none vs light vs heavy)
- Tone (professional vs casual vs inspirational)

**Format Elements**:

- Carousel vs single image
- Video vs image
- Short-form vs long-form video
- Text post vs visual post

**Testing Methodology**:

```
Week 1-2: Test variable A vs B (same audience)
Week 3-4: Implement winner, test next variable
Week 5-6: Compound winning elements
Week 7-8: Scale winning combination
```
