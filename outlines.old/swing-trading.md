### H2: Advantages and Disadvantages of Swing Trading

**Advantages:**
- Less time-intensive than scalping/intraday
- Lower transaction costs
- Better for part-time traders
- Captures trending moves
- Lower stress levels
- Tax benefits (if held >1 day, avoid speculative tag)

**Disadvantages:**
- Overnight and weekend risk
- Gap openings can trigger stop-losses
- STCG tax (20%) if held <1 year
- Requires patience
- Capital blocked for days
- False breakouts can cause losses
